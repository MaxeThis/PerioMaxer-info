## 2. Description of the App

PerioMaxer is a periodontal charting tool designed for dental professionals. The App provides voice-controlled and manual data entry for periodontal examinations, including probing depths, gingival margins, bleeding on probing, furcation involvement, tooth mobility, and related clinical measurements.
